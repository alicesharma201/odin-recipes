Brownies

Description

Brownies are the ultimate chocolate indulgence, combining the richness of a cake with the dense, chewy texture of a cookie. Their crackly top, gooey center, and deep cocoa flavor make them one of the most loved desserts around the world.

For this recipe, we are making irresistibly fudgy brownies with a glossy, crackly crust and a soft, melt-in-your-mouth center. A touch of vanilla enhances the chocolate flavor, while optional chocolate chips make every bite even more decadent.

Ingredients

For the Brownie Batter

  • ½ cup Unsalted Butter (melted)
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Dry Ingredients

  • ½ cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • ¾ cup All-Purpose Flour
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Baking Powder

Optional Add-ins

  • ½ cup Chocolate Chips or Chopped Dark Chocolate
  • ¼ cup Chopped Walnuts or Pecans

For the Topping

  • Extra Chocolate Chips (optional)
  • Dusting of Cocoa Powder or Powdered Sugar (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare the Oven and Pan

    • Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
    • Grease or line an 8×8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.

  2. Mix the Wet Ingredients

    • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and sugar until smooth.
    • Add the eggs one at a time, whisking well after each addition.
    • Stir in the vanilla extract.

  3. Combine the Dry Ingredients

    • In another bowl, mix together the cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder.
    • Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until no dry streaks remain.

  4. Add Chocolate and Nuts

    • Fold in the chocolate chips and chopped nuts if using.
    • Do not overmix, as this can make the brownies less fudgy.

  5. Bake the Brownies

    • Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
    • Sprinkle extra chocolate chips on top if desired.
    • Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  6. Cool and Serve

    • Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before slicing.
    • Dust with cocoa powder or powdered sugar if desired.
    • Serve on their own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
